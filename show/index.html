Usage: pyzettel show [OPTIONS] ID

  Show a zettel on the command line or in an editor.

Options:
  -t, --show-title / -T, --no-show-title
                                  Show title of zettel.
  -e, --editor / -cli, --cli      Open zettel in editor.
  -f, --show-filename / -F, --no-show-filename
                                  Show filename of zettel.
  --help                          Show this message and exit.
